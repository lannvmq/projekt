Japanese Cuisine

Japanese cuisine emphasizes fresh, seasonal ingredients and elegant simplicity. Popular dishes include sushi, sas- himi, and ramen. Meals often feature a balance of rice, fish, and vegetables, seasoned with soy sauce, miso, and other umami-rich flavors.
